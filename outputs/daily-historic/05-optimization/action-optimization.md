# Optimization Action Plan - Historiq

**Created:** March 25, 2026
**Activates:** May 20, 2026 (launch day)

---

## Purpose

This document summarizes the ongoing ASO and app optimization strategy for Historiq after launch. The goal is to turn ASO from a launch-day task into a continuous improvement process.

---

## Optimization Cadence

| Frequency | Time Required | Focus |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Daily | 15-20 min | Reviews, crashes, download monitoring |
| Weekly | 1 hour (Mondays) | Keywords, CVR, competitors, sentiment |
| Bi-weekly | 1.5 hours | A/B tests, screenshots, promotional text |
| Monthly | 2-3 hours | ASO health audit, competitor deep dive, metadata review |
| Quarterly | 4-6 hours | Full keyword refresh, localization eval, strategic review |
| Annually | 8-10 hours | Complete audit, visual refresh, next-year strategy |

**Total ongoing commitment:** Approximately 2-3 hours per week.

---

## First 90 Days Post-Launch Plan

### Days 1-7 (May 20-26): Monitor and Respond
- Respond to every review within 24 hours
- Monitor crash rate obsessively (target 99.5%+)
- Track keyword rankings for 8 target keywords
- Identify top 3 user complaints
- Ship v1.1.0 hotfix if critical bugs found

### Days 8-30 (May 27 - June 18): Optimize
- Analyze first month data: downloads, CVR, retention
- A/B test first screenshot on Google Play
- Refine keywords based on actual ranking data
- Update promotional text (Apple) based on user feedback themes
- Ship v1.1.0 with bug fixes and quick wins

### Days 31-60 (June 19 - July 18): Expand
- Evaluate localization opportunity (Spanish, German, French)
- Run A/B test on app icon (Google Play)
- Plan and create seasonal content (July 4th focus)
- Analyze competitor responses to Historiq's launch
- Ship v1.2.0 with top feature requests

### Days 61-90 (July 19 - August 17): Refine
- Full keyword research refresh
- Major metadata update based on 90 days of data
- Screenshot redesign if CVR below target
- Evaluate in-app rating prompt timing
- Quarterly ASO report with next-quarter plan
