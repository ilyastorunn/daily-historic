`history,historical,events,today,daily,learn,education,facts,timeline,trivia,ancient,medieval,culture`

**Rationale:**
- `history` and `historical` — highest-volume terms in the US App Store for this category

## Summary: Cross-Locale Keyword Strategy Observations

### Universal High-Value Terms
Every locale should have equivalents of: **history**, **today**, **learn**, **education**, **knowledge**, **ancient**, **culture**. These are the backbone across all markets.

### Market-Specific Asymmetric Bets
The following locale-specific terms offer the highest relative opportunity versus competitors:

| Locale | Key Asymmetric Term | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| de-DE | `mittelalter` | Massive fantasy/RPG crossover; underused by history apps |
| fr-FR | `frise` | School-specific timeline term; low competition |
| sv | `quizfrågor` | Quiz intent is huge in Sweden; history apps ignore it |
| ja | `雑学` | Giant category in Japan; history apps rarely target it |
| ko | `상식` | High-volume general knowledge term; history apps miss it |
