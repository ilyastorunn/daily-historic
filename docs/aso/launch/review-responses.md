## Response Best Practices

1. **Respond within 24 hours** -- faster for negative reviews (4-hour target)
2. **Use their name** if visible -- personalization matters
3. **Reference specific details** from their review -- show you read it
4. **Acknowledge before solving** -- validate their experience first
5. **Provide actionable next steps** -- version numbers, workarounds, contact info
6. **Stay professional with rude reviews** -- other users read your responses
7. **Include support email** for complex issues -- move the conversation offline
8. **Track recurring themes** -- if 5+ reviews mention the same issue, it is a priority fix
9. **Update responses when fixed** -- reply again after shipping the fix
10. **Never argue** -- even when the user is wrong, redirect constructively

---

## Review Escalation Protocol

**Critical (respond within 2 hours):**
- Data loss reports
- Security or privacy concerns
- Payment issues (when IAP is live)
- Historically inaccurate content causing offense

**High Priority (respond within 4 hours):**
- App crashes consistently
- Core feature completely broken
- Review from someone with large following
- Negative review with many "helpful" votes

**Standard (respond within 24 hours):**
- Feature requests
- Minor bugs
- General feedback
- Positive reviews

**No Response Needed:**
- Spam / irrelevant content (report instead)
- Reviews with no actionable content ("bad app" with no detail)
