Every morning, discover the historical events that unfolded on today's date across centuries and civilizations. From ancient battles to scientific breakthroughs, political upheavals to cultural milestones — Historiq curates personalized history for the genuinely curious.

WHY HISTORIQ

Most history apps dump a list of dates on you. Historiq is different. Your interests shape what you see. Prefer Renaissance art over medieval warfare? Your feed reflects that. Curious what happened on your birthday in 1066? The Time Machine takes you there in seconds.

History isn't just trivia. It's the story of how the world became what it is today — and Historiq makes that story feel personal.

PERFECT FOR

* History enthusiasts who want daily intellectual engagement
* Students researching specific periods or events
* Professionals who enjoy starting the day with something meaningful
* Anyone curious about the world and its past
* Teachers looking for engaging historical snapshots

WHAT PEOPLE ARE EXPLORING

Download Historiq and make every day a history lesson worth having.

## Implementation Instructions

### Step 1: Open App Store Connect
1. Go to https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. Navigate to My Apps
3. Select Historiq (com.ilyastorun.histora)
4. Click "App Information" in the left sidebar

### Step 2: Enter Localizable Metadata
Navigate to the English (U.S.) localization section under "Version Information."

1. **App Name (Title):** Paste `Historiq - History Daily` into the "Name" field
2. **Subtitle:** Paste `Daily Historical Events` into the "Subtitle" field
3. **Promotional Text:** Paste the promotional text above into "Promotional Text"
4. **Keywords:** Paste the keyword string above into the "Keywords" field (verify no trailing space)
5. **Description:** Paste the full description above into the "Description" field

### Step 3: Validate in App Store Connect
- Character counters appear inline — confirm all fields show green
- Use the "Preview" button to see how the listing appears on a 6.7" device
- Ensure no placeholder text remains

### Step 4: Submit
- Title, subtitle, keywords, and description require an app build submission to take effect
- Promotional text can be updated at any time without a new build

**Important notes:**
- Do not add spaces after commas in the keyword field — each space consumes a character
- Apple ignores keywords that appear in your title or subtitle, so avoid duplication
- Promotional text is ideal for seasonal campaigns (e.g., "This Week: Black Death, Moon Landing, Magna Carta")
